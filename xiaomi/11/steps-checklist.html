‹ Steps checklist
Manage Battery Settings
Settings > Apps > Manage apps > StepSetGo > Battery saver > select 'No restriction'.
Allow Autostart
Settings > Apps > Manage apps > StepSetGo > Allow Autostart.
Lock StepSetGo as recent app
Recent apps > Long tap on StepSetGo and press Lock
You could also try:
Remove Restrictions
Security > Battery > App Battery Saver > StepSetGo > No restriction
Alternatively, try this:
Settings > Battery & Performance > Manage Apps Battery Usage > Apps > StepSetGo > Choose “No Restrictions” under Background Settings
Enable background activity
Launch StepSetGo > Press Home button > Open your App list (recent apps, square or three-line button) > Pull the StepSetGo App preview slightly down to reveal two buttons above it > Press “Lock”
